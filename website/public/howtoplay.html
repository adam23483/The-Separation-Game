How to Play

In this game, your goal is to connect two soccer players through their shared history. Here's how to get started:

  • Start a challenge with two random players.
  • Build a link path connecting them via teammates, shared clubs, or managers.
  • Use filters or the search bar to explore players by league, team, and season.
  • Earn points based on the accuracy and speed of your connections.
  • Discover alternate paths and learn interesting player connections.
